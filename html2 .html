Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup blueberries

Directions:

  1. Mix flour and sugar together
  2. In a seperate bowl,mix together milk and egg
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients together
  4. Mix in Blueberries
  5. Bake at 350 degrees Fahreheit for 17 minutes

Notes:

For Lemon Blueberry Muffins,add 1 tbsp lemon zest
